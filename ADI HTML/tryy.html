Lecture Dates

The Junior Detective Squad, also known as the Detective Boys, is a group of young student detectives from the Detective Conan series. They are known for getting involved in mysterious cases and helping solve crimes alongside Conan Edogawa.

The group started when Ayumi Yoshida, Genta Kojima, and Mitsuhiko Tsuburaya began exploring small mysteries together, which eventually led them to real criminal investigations.

As time passed, they officially formed the Detective Boys and continued solving different cases during school activities and free time.

Each member plays an important role in the team: Conan provides brilliant detective skills and leads the group’s investigations, Ayumi supports everyone emotionally and keeps the group positive, Genta contributes strength and bravery in difficult situations, Mitsuhiko uses logical thinking to analyze clues, and Ai Haibara adds scientific knowledge and sharp intelligence.

Despite being young, the group shows strong teamwork, curiosity, and courage, making them a memorable part of the Detective Conan series.

Conan Edogawa

Formerly famous teenage detective Shinichi Kudo, Conan was turned into a child after being poisoned by the Black Organization. To hide his identity, he lives as an elementary student and joins the Detective Boys. Conan is the true mastermind of the team. He quietly solves difficult cases using his intelligence, deduction skills, and gadgets from Professor Agasa while guiding the others without revealing his secret.

Ayumi Yoshida

Ayumi is a cheerful, sweet, and caring girl who admires Conan and enjoys detective activities. She is emotionally mature for her age and deeply values friendship. She acts as the emotional center of the Detective Boys, encouraging teamwork and positivity. Her kindness and observations sometimes help uncover important clues.

Ai Haibara

Ai is a talented and intelligent girl who is a member of the Junior Detective Squad. She is known for her sharp mind and ability to solve complex puzzles. She provides intellectual support and helps with investigations that require analytical thinking.

Genta Kojima

Genta is a confident and athletic boy who is a member of the Junior Detective Squad. He is known for his strength and determination. He provides physical support in investigations that require brute force or endurance.

Mitsuhiko Tsuburaya

Mitsuhiko is a smart and curious boy who is a member of the Junior Detective Squad. He is known for his intelligence and love of solving puzzles. He provides intellectual support and helps with investigations that require analytical thinking.
