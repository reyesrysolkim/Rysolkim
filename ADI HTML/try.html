Meet The Squad

Conan Edogawa

Conan Edogawa is the brilliant leader of the Junior Detective Squad. Although he appears to be a child, he possesses exceptional detective skills and intelligence. Conan always protects his friends while secretly solving dangerous cases.

Ai Haibara

Ai Haibara is calm, smart, and mysterious. She is highly intelligent and often helps Conan during investigations. Despite her quiet personality, she deeply cares about the Junior Detective Squad.

Ayumi Yoshida

Ayumi Yoshida is cheerful, kind, and brave. She enjoys going on adventures with the squad and always tries her best to help during investigations. Her positive attitude inspires the team.

Genta Kojima

Genta Kojima is energetic and fearless. He loves food and excitement, and he never backs down from a challenge. Genta adds strength and confidence to the Junior Detective Squad.

Mitsuhiko Tsuburaya

Mitsuhiko Tsuburaya is intelligent and curious. He enjoys science and technology, making him very useful during investigations. His logical thinking often helps the team solve mysteries.
